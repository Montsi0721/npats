Simple Process

From application
to collection

Four steps is all it takes — visit an office, track online, get notified, and collect your passport.

Step 01

Create Your Account

Register on NPATS in minutes. Your account is your portal to every application you make.

Step 02

Visit a Passport Office

Submit your physical documents at any authorised office. Officers link your application to your account.

Step 03

Track Every Stage

Follow your passport in real time from submission through biometrics, production and verification.

Step 04

Collect & Go

Get notified the moment your passport is ready. Pick it up at the designated collection point.
